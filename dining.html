Dining

The essentials: what’s good, what’s convenient, and what to plan ahead for.

Friday & Saturday (Wedding Events)

  • Friday 4–6 PM: Welcome cocktail party (apps + carving station)
  • Saturday 5 PM: Wine + apps + violin
  • Saturday 6:30 PM: Dinner
Bottom line
You won’t need to plan dinner Friday or Saturday night — we’ve got you.

The Oasis (On-site)

If you want easy, this is the move. It’s also the most “no-stress” option in the whole park.

  • The Inn Dining Room: more formal / sit-down
  • The Ranch: casual options
  • Bars: yes, order the prickly pear margarita

Tip: reservations can help during busy times.

Plan Ahead

Death Valley is remote. Food options exist, but they’re not “walk outside and choose from 20 restaurants.”

  • If you’re picky, plan meals at the resort
  • If you’re adventurous, you’ll be totally fine
  • Bring snacks (you’ll thank yourself)

You are not going to starve. You are also not in LA.

Snacks & Survival (Highly Recommended)

We want you hydrated, happy, and not hangry in the desert. A few easy wins:

  • Throw a few snacks in your bag before exploring
  • Bring water in the car
  • Eat something before the ceremony caravan
Our official snack stance
Snacks are always appropriate. Always.
