Ceremony Information

Everything you need to get there, be comfortable, and not stress.

Where & When

The ceremony will be held in Death Valley National Park at a location called Hole in the Wall.

  • Ceremony begins: 2:00 PM (please arrive by 1:45 PM)
  • Drive time from the Inn: ~25 minutes
  • Road: dirt/gravel (standard vehicles are fine)
No cell service at the ceremony site.
Download offline maps before you head into the park, or ride with someone who has them.

GPS

Coordinates: 36.4144, -116.7238

Tip: copy/paste into your map app ahead of time while you still have service.

Key Timing

  • 12:45 PM: Meet at Oasis Inn lobby
  • 1:00 PM: Caravan departs
  • ~1:30 PM: Arrive at Hole in the Wall
  • 2:00 PM: Ceremony begins

Turn-by-turn Directions (from The Inn)

  1. Leave the Inn and head East on CA-190 (left turn out of the parking lot).
  2. Drive about 5.25 miles on CA-190.
  3. Hole in the Wall Road will be on your left (it’s easy to miss).
  4. Turn left onto Hole in the Wall Road.
  5. Continue about 3.7 miles to the ceremony area.

Reference points from the guide: Twenty Mule Team Canyon sign (Hole in the Wall Rd is ~0.7 miles past), and the 1000 ft elevation sign just before the turn.

What to Bring

  • Water
  • Comfortable shoes (rocky ground)
  • Jacket or layer (desert temps can shift)
  • Sunglasses
Good news:
You don’t need to hike. It’s a short walk from parking to the ceremony spot.

Parking

Reserved sites: H5 and H6

  • H5: Primary parking (about 4 vehicles)
  • H6: Ceremony site & parking (about 1 vehicle)
  • Sites are ~0.5 miles apart; one vehicle will assist with shuttling if needed

Restrooms

  • Ceremony site: no restrooms or facilities — please use the restroom before leaving the hotel
  • Zabriskie Point: vault toilets, no running water
  • Artist’s Palette: vault toilets; you pass the hotel between Zabriskie and Artist’s Palette (recommended restroom stop)

If needed, there are private areas a short walk away from the ceremony site.

After the Ceremony

We’d love for everyone to stick around to enjoy the scenery and take photos with us.

  • ~3:00 PM: Hole in the Wall → Zabriskie Point (~10 min drive)
  • Then: Artist’s Palette (the spot that stole Zsanelle’s heart)
